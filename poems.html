My Original Poetry

Words woven together to paint emotions, experiences, and dreams.

Silence Speaks

In the quiet corners of my mind,
Echoes whisper truths I never find.
Silence isn’t empty, it’s full of sound,
Stories told when no one's around.

A Walk Alone

The road was empty, yet full of grace,
With every step, I found my place.
No need for company, I wasn’t lone,
For I had found myself—my very own.
